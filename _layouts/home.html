About me

With a Degree in Communication Design, I consider myself a person with good work ethic and good communication skills to work in a team and communicate with others in general.

I like to work on different areas of Design from editorial to digital as well as video editing, marketing and a lot more. Overall, I like to be creative.

I am a perfectionist through and through. I pay a lot of attention to detail and like to be 100% satisfied with everything I do.

My Work

  • Neon Rose, 2017

    This was a multimedia publication that was developed as a group project about the sixties counterculture and the Free Speech Movement. We tried to show how those revolutions are affecting our youth and the way they choose to protest and claim their rights nowadays.

  • First Things First, 2016

    First Things First is a typographic experiment that I did based on the First Things First manifesto, from 1964. I created two posters with one of the main teachings of the manifesto using only typography.

  • Inner City Blues, 2017

    This was another multimedia publication that was developed as a group project, once again about the sixties counterculture. This time we were challenged to create a "mixtape for revolution" - songs with a revolutionary purpose - and then create a publication around it.

  • Receita da Sopa da Pedra, 2016

    For this project I took a classic children's tale - Sopa da Pedra - and created illustrations for it. When designing the book I decided to give it a little twist to make it more fun to read and turned it into a make-believe recipe book.

  • Design Português - pós-revolução, 2017

    This group project is about the History of Design. My group and I decided to explore Portuguese Design - where did it come from? when was it born? who are the main references? - We ended up focusing on Designer José Brandão and Professor Aurelindo Jaime Ceia.

  • No Big Deal, 2018

    No Big Deal is a Web App that I created and designed as a project for my post-graduation in Digital Experience Design. It's purpose is to allow people who need certain everyday tasks done for them, and people with some spare time and in need of some money, to find and help each other out.
